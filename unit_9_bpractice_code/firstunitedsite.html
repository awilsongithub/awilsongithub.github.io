Firstunitedoakpark.com: Website Design (2012)

I led a user-centered redesign of the public website while working as office manager and webmaster for First United Church. I organized a survey of new members regarding the site, reviewed site analytics, consulted staff and engaged a consultant to audit the site. I proposed content updates, consolidation, more multi-media and a streamlined page structure to pastoral staff and was given a week off work to complete the work. Many newcomers to the Church reported their experience with the new site as an important reason for engaging with the Church.
